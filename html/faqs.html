Q: What is the Double-Blind Review Process?

A double-blind review process is one where the identity of the author of a submitted paper is concealed from the reviewer and the identity of the reviewer is likewise concealed from the author. This process was decided to be the fairest review process by the USJ leadership and will serve as an assurance of impartial paper selection for publication.
